About Me

I've tried everything — and that's exactly the point.

I'm Lucky Meena, an IT Specialist based in Vancouver, BC. I've worked across government, finance, and retail — from securing federal systems at BDC to deploying Oracle Xstore POS across 50+ Aritzia locations. I hold a B.A. in Psychology from Capilano University, because understanding people is just as important as understanding systems.

But I don't stop at the job description. I'm a MERN stack developer who's built revenue-generating products, a content creator streaming on YouTube and creating Instagram reels, and someone who deeply believes in critical thinking and the entrepreneurial mindset. Whether it's automating IT workflows with PowerShell or launching ObjectHunt.com to 100K+ users — I build things from zero.

My sign on the wall says it best: "Fearlessly Be Yourself."
